Project Portfolio

GAMES

PUTTY PARTY

Putty Party is a 2D co-operative puzzle-platformer being developed as part of my final year project at Swinburne.

I am the lead programmer and project manager on a team of 9 students, and we have been working tirelessly since March to get the game ready for showing at PAX Australia later this year.

FACE INVADERS

Face Invaders is a fast-paced arcade survival game for iOS, Android, and WP8 that casts you as the main character.

I developed it as part of Electric Mammoth Studios on a casual schedule over roughly two years.

I was the only programmer on the project, working closely with our designer/producer and artists to put the game together.

PARTNERS IN CRIME

Partners in Crime is an asymmetric co-operative stealth game developed as part of subject Games Lab 2 at Swinburne. Created over one semester, it featured a combination of platforming and environmental hacking to circumvent security measures that seek to thwart you.

I was the lead programmer on a team of 3 programmers working alongside 2 artists.

THE ISLAND OF FUNK

The Island of Funk is a free-to-play browser-based arcade game about dancing your way to a high score on a desert island.

Released by Electric Mammoth on Kongregate in 2012, The Island of Funk was developed by a team of over 7 developers on a casual basis.

WEBSITES

SWEET AND SOUR FORK

Sweet and Sour Fork is a WordPress blog I put together for a food blogger.

The theme was custom-developed using PHP and the transition from Blogger to WordPress necessitated the development of some custom plugins to migrate all important site data.

TECHNO MARKETING

During my time at Techno Marketing, I was responsible for replacing the company's old Joomla website with a new WordPress website.

In addition to preparing the website for maintenance by other staff, certain third-party plugins required expansion and modification in order to replace those previously used in the Joomla website.
