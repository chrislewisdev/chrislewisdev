This is a lightning talk I delivered at both LevelsConf and NDC Sydney in 2018. It takes a step back from the really big factors that impact your happiness at work, and focuses instead on the little things - using your keyboard as one example. This talk was a bunch of fun to write and deliver, particularly while I was still deep in the throes of mechanical keyboard obsession!
